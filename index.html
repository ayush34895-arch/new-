Common Questions

Frequently Asked

What is the typical cost of a home interior project?
Our interior design projects range from ₹800 to ₹3,000+ per sq ft depending on the materials, finishes, and complexity. We offer customized packages for every budget without compromising on quality.
How long does a complete home design project take?
A typical residential interior project takes 45–90 days depending on scope and size. Architectural design projects may take 3–6 months from concept to construction completion.
Do you offer Vastu consultation separately?
Yes, we offer standalone Vastu consultation services. We can also integrate Vastu principles into any ongoing design project at no additional cost.
Can I see 3D designs before execution begins?
Absolutely. We provide detailed photorealistic 3D renders and virtual walkthroughs of your space before any physical work begins — so you can approve, modify, and feel confident in the outcome.
Do you work outside Ambala?
Yes, we undertake projects across Haryana, Punjab, Delhi NCR, and other parts of India. Contact us to discuss your project location and requirements.

Our Story

Building Dreams,
One Space at a Time

Mishul Gupta Architects & Interior Designers was founded with a singular vision: to create spaces that don't just look beautiful, but truly enhance the lives of those who inhabit them.

Based in Ambala, Haryana, our studio has grown over 12 years into one of the region's most trusted names in luxury architecture and interior design — working on projects ranging from intimate residences to landmark commercial developments across Northern India.

What Guides Us

Design Philosophy

We believe great design is invisible — it feels natural, inevitable, and deeply personal. Every space we create is a reflection of the client's story, told through materials, light, and form.

Customized Approach

No two projects are alike at our studio. We reject templates. Every design begins with a blank canvas and a deep understanding of who you are and how you want to live.

Attention to Detail

From the sweep of a staircase to the grain of a marble slab — we obsess over every detail. Because luxury is not about excess; it is about perfection in the smallest elements.

Client-First Approach

Your vision drives everything. We are collaborators, not dictators. Our process ensures you are informed, involved, and delighted at every stage — from first sketch to final delivery.
